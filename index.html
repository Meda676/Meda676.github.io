Work experiences

Data Analyst
Apr 2018 - present

Experience as data analyst for the Vieri Racing team and other teams enrolled in the italian motorcycle championship (CIV). My job is about managing the sensor wiring system and analyzing the telemetry data of a racing bike with the aim of providing the rider and the team with significant information about the performance in the various laps. I manage with the team the setup of the motorbike in terms of suspension adjustments and different ECU maps for engine management in order to change the behavior of the bike in the critical points of the track. To perform this work I use some commercial telemetry software (Danas I2M, 2D Datarecording, MOTEC), proprietary software of the various motorcycle manufacturers (Kawasaki, Yamaha, BMW, Honda) and other software developed personally.
