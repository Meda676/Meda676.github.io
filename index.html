About myself

I am a PhD student and a researcher of the Information Engineering and Mathematics department, at the University of Siena.

I am currently involved in the AMPERE European project and my work concerns the study of software resiliency techniques for critical environments and its run-time implementation requirements.

I am a curious person, very organized and able to manage time productively.
My interests include motorcycles, technology, movies and cooking.
